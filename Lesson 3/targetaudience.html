Travelers

This target audience will want to know the current weather conditions especially on the specific town or place they are traveling. Some senarios that come to mind are the following:

  • If the weather is nice are there any activites to do in this specifictown?
  • Is there a storm warning that will disrupt any local acvities based onthat town?
  • If there is a blizzard or heavy snowfall is there any precautions thatneed to be taken?

I can see travelers useing this mostly on their phone to quickly see what how the weather is while traveling. I can see it being a mom or a dad traveling with their family, or a couple taking a romantic trip, or a group of college kids planning a trip on the weekend or during a week break.

Locals

This target is similar to the travelers but they aren't worried too much about the weather from other towns. The senarios come into to mind are the following:

  • How's the weather going to be in my town or county?
  • Is the weather nice enough to do any outdoor activites?
  • If it's snowing bad are the schools closed in my county?

I feel that majority of this audience are parents or people who are older than 30 and like the previous group, this website needs to be user friendly.
